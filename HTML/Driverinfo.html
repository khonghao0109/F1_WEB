Biography

Oscar Piastri is the real deal. A rookie in name when he entered Formula 1 in 2023, but a proven champion in the junior categories – and it shows.

The Australian raced to Formula 3 and Formula 2 championship glory in back-to-back seasons in 2020 and 2021, following in the footsteps of Ferrari star Charles Leclerc and Mercedes protege George Russell. But there was no next obvious step for him with Alpine.

Instead, after a high-profile contractual dispute, he landed at McLaren for 2023, where he took the challenge to highly-rated team mate Lando Norris, showing rapid adaptation and impressive maturity in his debut season, with a sprint victory in Qatar capping a fine freshman year.

His sophomore season in 2024 has been even more impressive, with Piastri establishing himself as a regular race winner and podium finisher. His partnership with Norris has propelled McLaren into championship contention, with the Australian showcasing exceptional racecraft and consistency.

Not content with being the sport's most talented rookie, Piastri has continued his meteoric rise and is now firmly established as one of Formula 1's brightest stars, with multiple Grand Prix victories to his name before his 24th birthday.

Cool, calm and collected, Piastri lets his driving do the talking – and based on his career so far, he's going to have plenty to say in the years to come.
